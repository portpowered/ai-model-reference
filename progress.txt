## Codebase Patterns
- Use `BlogRelatedDocs` with explicit `relatedDocIds` for blog MDX; it delegates to `RelatedRegistryDocs` with blog-specific empty/all-unavailable fallback copy and `testId="blog-related-docs"`. Pass `resolveOptions` in tests for injectable lookup.
- Use `RelatedRegistryDocs` with explicit `registryIds` for narrative surfaces; it delegates to `resolveRelatedRegistryDocs` and renders `docsChromeLinkClassName` links with empty, all-unavailable, and partial-unavailable fallbacks. Pass `resolveOptions` in tests for injectable lookup.
- Use `resolveRelatedRegistryDocs(registryIds)` in `related-registry-docs.ts` for explicit registry-id lists (blog `relatedDocIds`, narrative surfaces); it returns `available` link items and `unavailable` `missing`/`unpublished` entries while preserving input order. Inject `getRecordById` / `publishedRegistryIds` in tests like `related-docs.test.ts`.
- Reuse `related-registry-docs.test-fixtures.ts` for shared resolver/component/blog test records and `relatedRegistryDocsResolveOptions`; story-004 acceptance coverage lives in `related-registry-docs-behavior.test.tsx`.
- Classifier-free guidance concept page lives at `/docs/concepts/classifier-free-guidance`; the `classifier-free guidance` alias belongs on `concept.classifier-free-guidance`, not on `concept.conditioning`.
- Classifier-free guidance registry record uses `classification.concept.inference`, `conceptType: inference`, and `generation-and-diffusion` sidebar grouping; `relatedIds` should stay limited to conditioning, diffusion-model, denoising-generation, `model.clip`, and `paper.latent-diffusion`.
- Concept registry ids route to `/docs/concepts/<slug>` only after adding to `PUBLISHED_CONCEPT_SECTION_REGISTRY_IDS` in `published-docs-registry-ids.ts`; until then glossary pages at `/docs/glossary/<slug>` remain the live surface.
